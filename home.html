About Us

Our Mission

At Meow's Home, our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, and rehome pets in need. We believe every pet deserves a loving family and a safe home. Since 2015, we've successfully matched over 5,000 pets with their forever families.

Our Values

  • Compassion: We treat every animal with love and care
  • Integrity: We ensure responsible pet ownership through thorough screening
  • Transparency: We're honest about each pet's health and behavior
  • Community: We support pet owners throughout their journey

Meet Our Team

Our dedicated team of animal lovers works tirelessly to ensure every pet receives proper care, veterinary attention, and finds their perfect match. We're supported by passionate volunteers from the community.
